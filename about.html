My Story.

Hi, I’m Sefray, I had a dream: to play any hard game on a livestream, blind run, with one life/game, where one death = fail☠️ I thought it could be engaging, intense, fun, entertaining! But I was sick, broke, and wanted to stay anonymous, so I did not do it for many years. But I decided to share my idea with people on X, just to have fun watching others doing it and feel myself less worthless. Nobody cared.

My hope and vision.

I hope with the success of the site and community, I will be able to earn enough money to go the US and get medical treatment. Thank you God and all the good people involved.

The Goal of the Site.

To have the most fun out of games, for people to challenge themselves, connect with others, have extra entertainment for viewers. To inspire the philosophy of "You only live once - so make it count and try your best to live a good life to the fullest."

Key Moments

  • 27.06.2025: Tried sharing it on X. No response. Came up with the idea for the site. Started creating the site, on and off, with Grok.
